User & Privacy Guide

1. Why are API Credentials Required?

To search and retrieve paper abstracts at scale without triggering security blocks, academic search engines require user identification. Identifying yourself places your requests in prioritized "polite pools" rather than anonymous shared pools.

2. Getting Free API Credentials

  • OpenAlex & Crossref Polite Emails: No signup or accounts are required. Just use your valid academic or professional email.
  • CORE API Key: 100% Free. Register at core.ac.uk/services/api and generate a key.
  • Semantic Scholar API Key: Optional. Leave it blank to use the public unauthenticated search tier.

3. Privacy & Security Guarantee

Your data remains completely private.
  • Local Storage: Keys and email settings are saved strictly inside your local browser's localStorage database. They never leave your computer.
  • In-Memory Processing: Uploaded files are resolved in-memory on your local machine and never written to permanent remote disks.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: No telemetry, analytic tracking, or data sharing occurs. Queries are sent directly to the official academic APIs.
